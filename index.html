The Idea

  This software was created in order to assist the UNC Center of Learning and Disability with a particular study aimed to evaluate girl's with Rett Syndrome and hopefully prove that these girls are capable of so much more than previously imagined.

EyeReader

  EyeReader is software developed for the following purposes:
  • Capture gaze and fixation data points from the Tobii PCEye Go, and
  • Automate saving of associated video screen capture, heat map, and gaze plot generated by the Tobii Gaze Viewer

Dropbox

  Use Dropbox as a means of:

  • Delivering software to users, and
  • Gathering data from users
